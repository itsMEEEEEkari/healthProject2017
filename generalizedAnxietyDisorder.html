{Generalized Anxiety Disorder}

Generalized Anxiety Disorder, also known as GAD, is a common form of anxiety that causes a person to worry excessively over many things. A person with this disorder will also find it hard to talk about their worries, and usually cannot explain them.This Disorder will cause a person to worry about everything, even something as simple as getting up in the morning.When they are very anxious, a person with GAD may also get headaches and stomachaches, physical symptoms of the disorder.The constant cycle of worry that GAD causes makes individuals with this disorder feel that they are not in control of their worry, which causes more anxiety and feeds the cycle.

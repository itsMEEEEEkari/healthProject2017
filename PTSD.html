{Post Traumatic Stress Disorder}

Post Traumatic Stress Disorder is an anxiety disorder that occurs after a person has been in a traumatic event, like a car accident or a natural disaster. Its causes this person to have flashbacks and nightmares of the event.

Home Generalized Anxiety Disorder Panic Disorder Phobia Disorders PTSD Social Anxiety Disorder Obsessive Compulsive Disorder

Signs and Symptoms

Flashbacks

Nightmares

Intrusive memories

Avoiding people and places that remind one of the traumatic event

Startle easily

Tense feeling

Difficulty sleeping

Anger issues

Statistics

Almost 3% of the adult population of the US suffers from PTSD

67% of people who experience traumatic events develop PTSD

Help

Psychotherapy:

  • Cognitive Behovior Therapy
  • Exposure Therapy

Medication:

  • Antidepressants
  • Possibly Prazosin

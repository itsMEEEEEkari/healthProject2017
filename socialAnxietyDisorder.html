{Social Anxiety Disorder}

Social Anxiety is an anxiety or phobia disorder related to social situations or interaction with other people. A person with social anxiety may also experience panic attacks in stressful social situations, and may also have a fear of having their disorder recognized.

Home Generalized Anxiety Disorder Panic Disorder Phobia Disorders PTSD Social Anxiety Disorder

Signs and Symptoms

An unreasonable fear of being judged or publicly humiliated

Few friends

Struggle to create healthy relationships

Obsessively worried about offending someone

Statistics

25% of teens experience social anxiety on some level

15 million american adults suffer from social anxiety

Only one in three people suffering from social anxiety seek help

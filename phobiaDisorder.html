{Phobia Disorders}

Phobia Disorder covers a variety of very specific phobias, like arachnophobia or claustrophobia. People with phobia disorders tend to avoid situations in which they have to face their fear, and this can get in the way of their day to day life. Phobia disorders can also cause panic attacks.

Signs and Symptoms

An intense, irrational fear of something

Racing heart

Nausea

Shortness of breath

Rapid speech

Higher blood pressure

Shaking or trembling

Chest pain

Lightheadedness

Sweating

Fear of impending doom

Choking sensation
